About me

Ciao! I’m a designer driven by curiosity and a love for intuitive, user-centered design. I blend logic with creativity to craft impactful solutions that resonate and inspire.

I have worked across diverse industries, from state-owned enterprises and leading technology companies to education pioneers and startups, helping to build intuitive digital experiences and optimize user journeys. My roles have ranged from interaction and user research, focusing on enhancing user satisfaction and achieving business results through user-centric solutions.

I am currently a master's student in Digital & Interaction Design at Politecnico di Milano, where I have gained expertise in user experience design. My studies have covered areas such as prototyping, usability testing, design thinking, and immersive technologies. I have also explored the use of tools like Arduino, Touch Designer, and various UX research methodologies to create user-centered solutions that bridge the gap between technology and human experience.
